FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the Typewriting courses?

Our Telugu and English Typewriting courses typically span 3 to 6 months depending on your skill level and certification requirement.

Will I get a government-recognized certificate after completing the course?

Yes. On successful completion, you will receive a government-approved Typewriting certificate which is valuable for jobs and official applications.

Do I need prior experience to join the Typewriting course?

No prior experience is required. We offer beginner-friendly modules that guide you from basics to advanced typing proficiency.

What computer courses do you offer?

We offer Basic Computer courses covering MS Office, Internet usage, and typing, as well as Advanced courses including programming, graphic design, and accounting software.

Are your computer courses suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed for both beginners and advanced learners, with personalized guidance to suit all levels.

Do you provide practical hands-on training?

Yes, we emphasize practical training. Our students work on real-world projects and assignments to build strong practical skills.

Will I get a certificate after completing computer courses?

Yes, on completion, you will receive a certificate recognized by reputed institutions and useful for career opportunities.

Can I attend classes online or is it only offline?

We offer both offline and online classes to provide flexible learning options as per your convenience.
